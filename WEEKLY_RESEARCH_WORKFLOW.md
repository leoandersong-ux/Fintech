# Weekly Research Workflow

This is the operating rhythm for the Zambia Digital Lending Ops Radar personal research platform.

## Monday: Refresh Public Sources

- Run selected official and competitor public sources.
- Check `source-health` before adding new sources.
- Do not enable app listings or review-heavy sources unless the public-data boundary is clear.

## Tuesday: Review New Signals

- Use the Streamlit `新信号 New` and `复核 Queue` tabs.
- Prioritize signals with high review need or high lending relevance.
- Keep source facts, personal interpretation, and next action separate.

## Wednesday: Update Competitor Matrix

- Generate the competitor product matrix.
- Compare positioning, product layer, limit tier, speed promise, payment maturity, support/privacy maturity, and evidence gaps.
- Treat completeness scores as research-field completeness, not product quality.

## Thursday: Write Business Interpretation

- Use the `业务解读 Intelligence` tab.
- Convert source facts into:
  - affected lending process,
  - possible operational impact,
  - recommended research action,
  - follow-up question.

## Friday: Generate Weekly Notes and Sync Milestone Changes

- Generate weekly personal notes.
- Generate signal quality and competitor matrix snapshots.
- Run compile checks.
- Scan the intended sync set for secrets, private borrower data, employer-internal data, and unrelated project assets.
- Push milestone updates to `leoandersong-ux/Fintech`.

## Rule Of Thumb

More sources are not the goal. Better judgment is the goal.
